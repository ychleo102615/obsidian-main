# ephemeral #card  
[[epi- "on, over, upon"]] + [[*Hehmer- "day"]] + -al  
短暫的、轉瞬即逝的；只存在極短時間  
想像一朵清晨盛開、傍晚凋謝的花——不是「消失了」，而是「原本就只為這一天而生」  
The brand's viral moment was entirely ephemeral—within a week, the conversation had moved on.
