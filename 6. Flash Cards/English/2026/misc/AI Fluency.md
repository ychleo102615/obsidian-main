# AI Fluency 
# diligence #card  
dis- + [[*leg- (1) "to collect, gather,"]] → *diligere* (珍愛、精心挑選)  
謹慎而持續的投入；法律語境中指應盡的注意義務（due diligence）  
珍愛 → 用心 → 謹慎 → 不懈努力：因在乎而持續謹慎地投入  
*We must apply due diligence when evaluating AI outputs for accuracy and bias.*  
^1773247090206

# competence #card  
com- + [[pet- "to rush, to fly"]] → *competere* (各方共同趨向同一目標 → 恰好對齊)  
具備充分能力以勝任某事；整體的適任狀態  
「共同趨向同一點」→ 你與任務之間恰好對齊，不多不少  
*Evaluating AI competence means assessing whether it meets the threshold for a given task.*  
^1773247090215

# competency #card  
同 competence；-cy 強調可具體定義的名詞形  
可評量的特定技能項目；常以複數 competencies 出現，指可列舉的能力清單  
competence 是狀態，competency 是單位（可被定義、被測量）  
*Core AI competencies include prompt engineering, critical evaluation, and output verification.*  
^1773247090218

# disseminate #card  
dis- (in every direction) + seminare (播種) ← [[*sē- "to sow"]]  
廣泛散布資訊或觀點，帶有主動向四方傳播的意圖  
像播種一樣把想法撒向四方，期望各處生根  
*Researchers have a responsibility to disseminate AI findings transparently to the public.*  
^1773247090221
