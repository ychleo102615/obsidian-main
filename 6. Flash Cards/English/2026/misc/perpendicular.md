# perpendicular #card
[[*pend- "to hang, weigh"]] (per- thoroughly)  
垂直的；成直角的；完全與地平面成直角  
想像一條繩子綁著重物自然垂直下擺——那就是 perpendicular 的原始意象  
The building's foundation must be perpendicular to the ground for structural stability.
