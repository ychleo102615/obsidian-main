#### Chain
```html
<filter
      id="drop-shadow"
      x="-100%"
      y="-100%"
      width="300%"
      height="300%"
    >
      <feGaussianBlur in="SourceAlpha" stdDeviation="4" />

      </feMerge>
```

## Patterns
fill 可以用 pattern 來組合。

```css
<svg
  viewBox="0 0 32 32"
  width="256"
  height="256"
>
  <defs>
    <pattern
      id="grid-pattern"
      width="2"
      height="2"
      patternUnits="userSpaceOnUse"
    >
      <path
        d="
          M 0,0
          L 2,0
          L 2,2
        "
        fill="none"
        stroke="white"
        stroke-width="0.1"
      />
    </pattern>
  </defs>
  
  <rect
    width="32"
    height="32"
    fill="url(#grid-pattern)"
  />
  
  <!--
    The grid fills in the top/right sides of
    each cell, which means the left and bottom
    edges of the grid are left empty. Fill them
    in as well:
  -->
  <path
    d="
      M 0,0
      L 0,32
      L 32,32
    "
    fill="none"
    stroke="white"
    stroke-width="0.1"
  />
</svg>
```


用法像是 `background-repeat`
